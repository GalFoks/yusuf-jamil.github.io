02 / CAUSAL INFERENCE · JUNE 2026
Causal Impact of Platform Discounts on Seller Margin
PythonSQL OLSIV/2SLS DiDstatsmodels

Does funding platform discounts erode seller margin? Ran a full causal pipeline — OLS with clustered errors, Hausman test, IV/2SLS with two instruments, and DiD with a placebo check — on 1,873 active sellers and 112,101 transactions (Olist dataset). Result: no significant effect found; placebo passed (p=0.74). Delivered with a constrained-optimization pricing model and 26-page report.
